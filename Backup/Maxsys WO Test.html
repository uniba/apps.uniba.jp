In the air, on the ground, at sea or in space, many of today's successful military aerospace programs have one thing in common: Maxsys automated test solutions. Maxsys combines innovative system solution development with state-of-the-art fixturing and software design, paving the way for superior performance and reliability of today's complex, technically advanced military aerospace equipment.


We've designed sophisticated test solutions for a broad spectrum of applications including fire control systems and avionics, airborne surveillance, mobile and fixed air defense systems, sensors, and integrated fire control and weapons delivery systems.


Standalone or networked, Maxsys automated test solutions routinely meet or exceed performance requirements. Where possible, we use open architecture platforms, subscribe to industry standards and use COTS components to maximize reliability and upgradeability.
